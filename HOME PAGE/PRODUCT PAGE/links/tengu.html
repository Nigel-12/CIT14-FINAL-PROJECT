The Geekvape Tengu RDA is a well made dual 24mm dripper that encompasses a deep juice well and velocity style build posts. The Geekvape Tengu RDA was sent to me in the standard card sample box; this is different to the retail packaging! The retail version will likely include more items than mine (mine didn’t have any paperwork with it).



  • Size: 24×28.3mm.
  • Side adjustable airflow.
  • Solid 510 pin and BF squonk pin included.
  • Dual coil deck.
  • 6mm deep juice well.
  • 810 Delrin and PMMA drip tips included.
