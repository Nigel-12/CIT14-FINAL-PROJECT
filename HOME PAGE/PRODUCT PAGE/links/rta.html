Named for the ruler of the Olympian Gods, the Zeus RTA is Geek Vape’s newest creation. Designed for tank lovers, this ambitious platform is the brainchild of Geek Vape’s GM, Justin and boasts a “leak-proof design” and 3D airflow control. The simple lines and eye-catching “Zeus” imprint on the inner chamber is complimented by a larger drip tip and inconspicuous airflow control ring.



  • 25mm Diameter.
  • 4.5mL Capacity.
  • Elevated Postless,
  • SingleConfigurations.
  • Side-Secured via Flathead Slotted Screws.
  • PEEK Insulator.
