The Geekvape Aegis Boost is the first AIO by popular manufacturer Geekvape, and the second pod style vape after the Bident. Geekvape started by making some of the best rebuildables out there, and then went on to make mods and made some of the best ones as well. They also produced one of the best sub ohm tanks in the Alpha so, in short, yeah… Geekvape has been killing it.
