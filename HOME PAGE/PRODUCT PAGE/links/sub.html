The Zeus Tank by GeekVape is a sophisticated sub-ohm system with a 5ml vape juice capacity with a 3.5ml spare tank glass option. Featuring a threaded top cap with dual fill ports for easy re-filling and an adjustable top airflow which filters the air from the top, down an inner channel and up through the coil for really great flavor production, enabling you to tailor your vape to the perfect settings.



  • 26mm Diameter.
  • 5mL Capacity.
  • OCC
  • Threaded Top Fill System with Dual Fill Ports
  • Side-Secured via crosshead Slotted Screws.
  • PEEK Insulator.
